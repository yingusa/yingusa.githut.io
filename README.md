## 📍 Port Details & Highlights

### 🇧🇸 Nassau, Bahamas (Wednesday, June 3)
* **Time:** 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
* **Highlights:** Nassau is the vibrant capital of the Bahamas, located on New Providence Island. It features a blend of colonial architecture, bustling markets, and beautiful beaches.
* **Popular Activities:** 
  * Visiting Atlantis Paradise Island (Water Park, Marine Exhibits).
  * Snorkeling or diving in the vibrant coral reefs.
  * Shopping at the Nassau Straw Market.
  * Visiting the historic Queen's Staircase and Fort Fincastle.

### 🏝️ Half Moon Cay, Bahamas (Thursday, June 4)
* **Time:** 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM
* **Highlights:** Half Moon Cay is Carnival's award-winning private island destination. Known for its pristine, crescent-shaped white-sand beach and crystal-clear turquoise waters.
* **Popular Activities:**
  * Swimming, paddleboarding, or relaxing in beach cabanas.
  * Horseback riding on the beach and in the ocean.
  * Kayaking in Bonefish Lagoon.
  * Enjoying the complimentary island-style beach buffet.

### 🌊 Celebration Key, Bahamas (Friday, June 5)
* **Time:** 7:00 AM – 1:00 PM
* **Highlights:** Celebration Key is Carnival's brand-new, exclusive destination located on Grand Bahama Island, designed specifically for Carnival guests.
* **Popular Activities:**
  * Relaxing by the massive lagoons and pristine beach areas.
  * Enjoying local Bahamian food, drinks, and shopping at the retail bazaar.
  * Family-friendly waterslides and play areas.
